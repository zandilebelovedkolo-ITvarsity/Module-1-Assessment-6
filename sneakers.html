Bathu Sneakers

Nice,Comfy & Suitable for any occasion

A Truly African Sneaker Brand

Bathu aims to be a sneaker brand that Africans can proudly affiliate with. A Sneaker Brand from Africa for Africa.

Bathu is passionate about energising Africans, helping them to help themselves and reigniting hope within African communitiesBathu shoes store is a unique, local sneaker brand that tells an authentic African story. Designed for comfort and functionality, it's one of the few brands with shoes made in South Africa for both adults and children. Bathu shoes come in various colours and editions, for example, their standard Mesh Edition, Mesh 2.0, Moja Edition, Journey Edition Suede and the Journey Edition Leather.

The sneakers come in various colours including navy blue, grey, pink and light blue, and are priced from R900 up to R1200

Bathu shoes are locally manufactured in Durban , with some of the components imported from Lesotho & the East . Bathu Shoes' long term vision is to have presence in Europe and South America , telling its' story in these parts of the world and be recognised and identified globally as a leading African shoe brand!
